Classic Tiramisu #### Ingredients: - 6 large egg yolks - 3/4 cup sugar - 1 cup mascarpone cheese - 1 1/2 cups heavy cream - 2 cups strong coffee, cooled - 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional) - 1 package ladyfingers - Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting #### Instructions: 1. In a large bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale. 2. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth. 3. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into the mascarpone mixture. 4. Combine coffee and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish. Dip each ladyfinger into the mixture briefly, then layer in a dish. 5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers. 6. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. 7. Dust with cocoa powder before serving. ### 2. Chocolate Lava Cake #### Ingredients: - 4 oz bittersweet chocolate, chopped - 1/2 cup unsalted butter - 1 cup powdered sugar - 2 large eggs - 2 large egg yolks - 1 tsp vanilla extract - 1/2 cup all-purpose flour - Pinch of salt #### Instructions: 1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease four ramekins and place on a baking sheet. 2. Melt chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl over simmering water. Remove from heat and whisk in sugar. 3. Add eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla, whisking until smooth. Stir in flour and salt until just combined. 4. Divide batter among ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes, until edges are set but centers are soft. 5. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates. Serve warm. ### 3. Lemon Bars #### Ingredients: - 1 cup all-purpose flour - 1/2 cup butter, softened - 1/4 cup powdered sugar - 2 large eggs - 1 cup granulated sugar - 2 tbsp all-purpose flour - 1/2 tsp baking powder - 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice - Powdered sugar, for dusting #### Instructions: 1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish. 2. In a bowl, combine flour, butter, and powdered sugar. Press into the bottom of the prepared dish. 3. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until lightly golden. 4. In another bowl, whisk eggs and granulated sugar until thick. Stir in flour, baking powder, and lemon juice. 5. Pour over the baked crust and bake for 20-25 minutes, until filling is set. 6. Cool completely, then dust with powdered sugar before cutting into squares.
