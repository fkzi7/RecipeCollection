Caprese Skewers Ingredients: 1 pint cherry tomatoes 1 package fresh mozzarella balls (about 8 oz) Fresh basil leaves (about 1 cup) 1/4 cup balsamic glaze Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Wash and dry the cherry tomatoes and fresh basil leaves. Drain the mozzarella balls. Thread a cherry tomato, a fresh basil leaf, and a mozzarella ball onto small skewers or toothpicks. Arrange the skewers on a serving platter. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the skewers and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately or chill in the fridge until ready to serve. Cooking Time: 10 minutes 2. Stuffed Mushrooms Ingredients: 12 large white mushrooms, stems removed 4 oz cream cheese, softened 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup breadcrumbs 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper, to taste 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (for garnish) Instructions: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Clean the mushrooms and remove the stems, setting them aside for later use. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, garlic powder, thyme, salt, and pepper until smooth. Chop the mushroom stems finely and mix them into the cream cheese filling. Spoon the mixture into the mushroom caps, pressing lightly to fill them evenly. Place the stuffed mushrooms on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving. Cooking Time: 25 minutes (including prep time)
